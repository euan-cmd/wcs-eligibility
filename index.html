UK Government Grant Eligibility Checker

Up to £500/socket

Check your eligibility in 2 minutes

The Workplace Charging Scheme (WCS) is an OZEV grant that covers up to 75% of the purchase and installation cost of EV charge points — up to £500 per socket, across a maximum of 40 sockets. Answer the questions below to find out if you qualify and estimate your grant value.

Question 1

What type of organisation are you?

The WCS is open to businesses, charities, and public sector bodies registered in the UK.

Question 2

Is your organisation registered and based in the UK?

You must have a UK registered address. The installation must also be at a UK site.

Question 3

Do you have dedicated off-street parking at your site?

Charge points must be installed in off-street parking associated with your business premises — a car park, yard, or similar. On-street parking does not qualify.

Question 4

Do you own the site, or do you have landlord permission to install charge points?

You must either own the parking area or have written permission from your landlord to install charge points.

Question 5

Does your organisation use eligible vehicles for business purposes?

At least one eligible vehicle must be used in connection with the business. Eligible types include: cars, motorcycles, mopeds, small vans (<3.5t), large vans (3.5–12t), taxis / private hire vehicles, minibuses, and HGVs.

Question 6

Have you previously claimed WCS grant funding at this site?

You cannot claim for charge points already installed before your application. However, you may claim additional sockets if you have not yet reached the 40-socket limit.

Question 7

How many sockets have you previously claimed WCS funding for?

Enter the number of sockets you already have under the WCS scheme. This determines how many more you can apply for (maximum 40 total).

Question 8

How many new charge point sockets are you planning to install?

Each socket is eligible for a grant of up to £500 (75% of cost). You can apply for up to 40 sockets in total across all WCS claims.

Eligibility Summary

Grant Estimate

Grant amount is indicative. Final grant is 75% of actual eligible costs, capped at £500 per socket. You must use an OZEV-authorised installer.
